FAQ

Questions
Answered

Still on the fence? These are the questions we get most often. If yours isn't here, WhatsApp us — we reply fast.

Ask on WhatsApp
Do I need to prepare anything before we start?
No preparation needed. Just bring access to your data sources (Excel files, database credentials, spreadsheets, or SaaS tool logins) and a clear sense of the decisions you want to make faster. We'll handle the rest on the discovery call.
How quickly can you deliver a first working version?
For standard projects, the first working version is typically delivered within 48–72 hours of receiving your data. Complex multi-source builds take 5–7 working days. We'll confirm timelines in writing before starting.
Do you work with businesses outside India?
Absolutely. We work with clients across the UK, US, UAE, Australia, and beyond. All collaboration happens over email, WhatsApp, and Zoom — timezone differences have never been a barrier to delivering quality work.
What if my data is messy or incomplete?
That's the norm — almost every client we work with starts with messy data. Data cleaning and structuring is a core part of what we do, not an extra. We'll assess the state of your data in the audit phase and include the cleanup in your scope.
Will my team be able to use the dashboards independently?
That's a core goal of every project. Every delivery includes a walkthrough session and written documentation so your team can filter, navigate, and interpret the dashboards without needing to call us. We build for self-sufficiency, not dependency.
Is my data kept secure and confidential?
Yes. We sign an NDA before any data is shared, we work within your own Power BI environment where possible, and we never store client data beyond the project scope. Data security and confidentiality are non-negotiable for us.
Can you connect to our CRM, Shopify, Google Ads, etc.?
Yes. We connect Power BI to HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, WooCommerce, Google Analytics 4, Google Ads, Meta Ads, SQL databases, Excel/CSV files, REST APIs, and more. If you use it to run your business, we can almost certainly connect to it.
