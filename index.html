Customer AnalyticsSegmentationLTV
Customer Analytics Dashboard
An e-commerce brand had thousands of customers but no systematic way to identify who was loyal, who was slipping, and who was churning silently.
⚠️
The Problem
No customer segmentation meant every buyer got the same communication. High-LTV customers were being treated identically to one-time purchasers. Churn was invisible until it was too late.
✅
The Impact
RFM segmentation model with live churn risk scoring and LTV cohort tracking. Retention campaigns targeted Champions and At-Risk separately. Repeat purchase rate improved 41% in 90 days.

⚠️
The Problem

✅
The Impact
Live operations dashboard tracking fulfilment time, defect rate, and inventory turns daily. On-time delivery rose from 61% to 87%. Fulfilment time cut from 3.2 to 1.8 days within 6 weeks.
